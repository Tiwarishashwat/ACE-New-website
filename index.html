TECH-WORKSHOPS

Workshops should be an integral part of our academic curriculum, as they not only provide us with abundant ledge on the subject in question, but also teach us the importance of team work, group discussions and sharing experiences. They teach us something outside the theoretical boundaries of our books. We at ACE, keeping academic excellence as a goal in mind, conduct regular intensive workshops for our students.

PROJECTS

Projects on various technological advancements and upcoming new technologies is a major focus in ACE student chapter.We provide a platform for students to embark on a journey of technological experimentation, leading to the birth of innovative ideas and successfully working models. Our future scope for the chapter is aimed at creating an environment rich in resources required for such growth.

ONLINE EVENTS

UPES ACE SC organises various online competitions ranging from technical and non-technical quizzes to fun events. Online Competitions not only help you in enhancing your skills but is also easy to participate in. Events that are held online reach large number of people and it turns out as a great way to showcase your potential talent to the world.
