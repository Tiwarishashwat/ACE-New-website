HackIT-21

Lets hack ideas for a better world !!

Description

  • This event is a two rounds event where participants have to choose a theme out of the provided ones. These themes will be based on Real World Scenarios.
  • After choosing a theme, the participants would be given a certain time period to think of an idea based on their chosen theme and submit the abstract of their idea. We will then review their ideas and select the Finalist (Top 10).
  • Finalists will then explain their project with the help of a presentation. Presentation can be in any form like video demo, PPT, Canva, etc, etc.
  • Participants must note that the ideas they present must be implementable and must not harm anyone (Nature, Animals, Humans) in anyway.

Important Details

  • Eligibility: All UPES Students are eligible
  • Teams Size: 1-4
  • Registration Fee: Free of cost

Rules

  • A student can be a part of only one application.
  • The event will take place in two rounds.
  • In the first round, participants will have to submit the abstract of their idea.
  • Your submission will then be evaluated and the top 10 ideas will be selected for the final round.
  • Final round will be the live pitching round. Selected finalists will present their solution in front of a panel of the judges. Presentation can be in any form (e.g. PPT, video demo, etc etc)
  • The Judge panel will be cross-questioning the participants in the final round.
  • After all the presentations, the top three winners will be declared.
  • The decision of judges will be final.
